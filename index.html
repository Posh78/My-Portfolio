About me

Osas Belinda Ogie. Born in Nigeria, live in the UK, graduated in masters of art in Community Leadership, and a Bachelor degree in Community and Social Care, Policy and Practice. Osas has been living in the UK since 2013. Before relocating to the UK, Osas lived in Italy for 17 years where she studied Classic Music and Vocal, and Health and Social Care. Osas has recently completed a course in Cyber Security level 2, and she is currently studying on Developing Coding level 2 with Code Nation. Osas also acquire a professional Certificate in Project Management, and has developed, and runs her own business over the last three years. Osas had 13 years’ work experience in health and social care, during this time she assisted and supported children, and young people, with autism spectrum, mental health, and physical disability, as well as elderly people with dementia and physical disability in intermedia care, and care homes. Assisting them to meet their day–to-day activities creating a homely atmosphere. Osas herself has also experienced a period of a long-term chronic illness during which she developed her own business. She is now studying to develop more skills to moving into a career in IT.
