About me

Osas Belinda Ogie. Born in Nigeria, and living in the UK since 2013. Osas graduated in the UK with masters of art in Community Leadership, and a Bachelor degree in Community and Social Care, Policy and Practice. Osas lived in Italy for 17 years where she studied Classic Music and Vocal, and Health and Social Care, before relocating to the UK. Osas run her own online retail business in the last three years and she is currently looking for a career change in IT. In 2023, Osas completed a course in Cyber Security level 2, a professional Certificate in Project Management, and now studying on Developing Coding level 2 with Code Nation, to acquire more skills and enhance her knowledge in IT to benefit her employer. Osas has 13 years’ work experience in health and social care. During this time she assisted and supported children, and young people, with autism spectrum, mental health, and physical disability, she also worked to assisting elderly people with dementia and physical disability in intermedia care, and care homes. Assisting them to meet their day–to-day needs, creating a homely atmosphere while promoting dignity, inclusion to improve health and wellbeing. Osas herself has experienced a period of a long-term chronic illness during which she developed and run her own business, and now looking to move into a career in IT.
