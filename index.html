About me

Osas Belinda Ogie. Born in Nigeria, and living in the UK since 2013. Osas graduated with masters of art in Community Leadership, and a Bachelor degree in Community and Social Care, Policy and Practice. Osas lived in Italy for 17 years where she studied Classic Music and Vocal, and Health and Social Care before relocating to the UK. Osas is currently looking to move into a career in IT, and she has recently completed a course in Cyber Security level 2, in 2023 and now studying on Developing Coding level 2 with Code Nation to acquire skills to enhance her knowledge in IT to benefit her employer. Osas also acquire a professional Certificate in Project Management, in 2023 and run her own business in the last three years. Osas had 13 years’ work experience in health and social care. During this time she assisted and supported children, and young people, with autism spectrum, mental health, and physical disability, she also worked to assisting elderly people with dementia and physical disability in intermedia care, and care homes. Assisting them to meet their day–to-day activities creating a homely atmosphere. Osas herself has also experienced a period of a long-term chronic illness during which she developed and run her own business, and now looking to move into a career in IT..
